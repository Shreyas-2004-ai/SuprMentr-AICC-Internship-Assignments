Assignment Name: Prompt Engineer
Description: Write prompts for resume, business idea, study plan and compare weak vs strong prompts

---

1. Introduction

Prompt engineering is the process of designing effective inputs (prompts) to get accurate and useful responses from AI models. A well-structured prompt gives better results than a vague one.

---

2. Objective

To compare weak and strong prompts and understand how prompt design affects AI output.

---

3. Prompt Comparisons

---

A. Resume Generation

Weak Prompt:
"Make my resume"

Problem:

* Too vague
* No details provided

Strong Prompt:
"Create a professional resume for a 3rd-year Computer Science student skilled in Python, JavaScript, React, and AWS. Include projects, skills, and internship experience in a clean format."

Improvement:

* Clear role
* Specific skills
* Structured output

---

B. Business Idea

Weak Prompt:
"Give me a business idea"

Problem:

* No domain or constraints
* Generic output

Strong Prompt:
"Suggest a unique startup idea in the food industry targeting college students in India, focusing on low cost and high scalability."

Improvement:

* Target audience
* Domain specified
* Constraints included

---

C. Study Plan

Weak Prompt:
"Make a study plan"

Problem:

* No subject or timeline

Strong Prompt:
"Create a 30-day study plan for learning Python and cloud computing for a beginner, including daily topics and practice tasks."

Improvement:

* Time-bound
* Subjects defined
* Structured output

---

4. Key Differences

* Weak prompts are vague and unclear
* Strong prompts are specific and detailed
* Strong prompts include context, constraints, and goals

---

5. Conclusion

Prompt engineering plays a crucial role in getting accurate results from AI systems. Well-designed prompts improve output quality and efficiency.
