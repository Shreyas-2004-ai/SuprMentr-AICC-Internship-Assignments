1. Introduction

In Natural Language Processing (NLP), text data is often messy and unstructured. It may contain slang, emojis, and spelling mistakes. Preprocessing is required to clean and prepare the text for analysis.

---

2. Objective

To collect messy sentences, identify issues like slang, emojis, and typos, and explain how to preprocess them.

4. Preprocessing Techniques

To clean the text, the following steps are used:

* Convert text to lowercase
* Remove emojis
* Correct spelling mistakes
* Expand slang words (e.g., "u" → "you")
* Remove special characters
* Remove extra repeated letters
* Tokenization (split words)

---

5. Conclusion

Text preprocessing is an important step in NLP. It helps convert messy and unstructured text into clean data, improving the performance of machine learning models.
