Assignment Name: Word Importance Explorer
Description: Use TF-IDF on 5 documents and identify top keywords

---

1. Introduction

TF-IDF (Term Frequency - Inverse Document Frequency) is a technique used to measure the importance of words in a document relative to a collection of documents.

---

2. Objective

To apply TF-IDF on a set of documents and identify important keywords.

---

3. Dataset

Five documents related to machine learning and data science were used.

---

4. Working

* TF (Term Frequency): Measures how frequently a word appears in a document
* IDF (Inverse Document Frequency): Measures how unique a word is across all documents
* TF-IDF Score = TF × IDF

Words with higher TF-IDF scores are more important.

---

5. Results

Each document has different top keywords:

Example:

* Document 1 → "amazing", "powerful", "learning"
* Document 2 → "subset", "deep", "learning"

These words are important because they are unique and meaningful in that document.

---

6. Conclusion

TF-IDF helps identify important words in documents. It is widely used in search engines, text analysis, and NLP applications.
