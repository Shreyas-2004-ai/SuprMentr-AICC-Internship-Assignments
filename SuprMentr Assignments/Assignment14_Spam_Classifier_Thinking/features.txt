Features for Spam Detection:

1. Presence of keywords:
   Words like "free", "win", "offer", "urgent", "click here"

2. Message length:
   Spam messages are often longer or unusually short

3. Use of special characters:
   Excessive use of !!! $$$ ###

4. Links in message:
   Presence of URLs or suspicious links

5. Sender information:
   Unknown or suspicious sender

6. Capital letters:
   FULL CAPS words often indicate spam

7. Frequency of messages:
   Repeated messages from same sender
