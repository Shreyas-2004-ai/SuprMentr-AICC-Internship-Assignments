Title: Study Hours vs Marks Prediction

Objective:
To create a dataset and analyze the relationship between study hours and student marks using machine learning.

Dataset:
The dataset contains two variables:
1. Study Hours (Feature/Input)
2. Marks (Label/Output)

Feature:
Study Hours represent the independent variable.

Label:
Marks represent the dependent variable.

Relationship:
There is a positive linear relationship between study hours and marks. As study hours increase, marks also increase.

Model Used:
Linear Regression is used to predict marks based on study hours.

Conclusion:
The model successfully predicts marks for given study hours, showing that increased study time leads to better performance.
