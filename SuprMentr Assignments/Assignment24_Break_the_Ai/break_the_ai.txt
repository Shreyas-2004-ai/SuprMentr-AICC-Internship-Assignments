Assignment Name: Break the AI
Description: Try confusing an LLM with tricky prompts and document results

---

1. Introduction

Large Language Models (LLMs) are powerful AI systems that understand and generate human-like text. However, they can sometimes produce incorrect or confusing responses when given tricky prompts.

---

2. Objective

To test the limitations of an AI model by giving challenging prompts and analyzing its responses.

---

3. Tricky Prompts and Observations

4. Prompt: "If a plane crashes on the border of two countries, where are the survivors buried?"
   Response: Survivors are not buried.
   Observation: Correct logical reasoning.

5. Prompt: "Which is heavier, 1 kg of iron or 1 kg of cotton?"
   Response: Both are equal in weight.
   Observation: AI handled the trick correctly.

6. Prompt: "Can you divide 10 by 0?"
   Response: Division by zero is undefined.
   Observation: Correct mathematical understanding.

7. Prompt: "Repeat the word 'hello' 1000 times."
   Response: AI may limit output or refuse.
   Observation: AI has response length restrictions.

8. Prompt: "Write a sentence that is both true and false at the same time."
   Response: May give paradox (e.g., "This sentence is false").
   Observation: Shows handling of logical paradox.

9. Prompt: "Translate this: 'Colorless green ideas sleep furiously.'"
   Response: Grammatically correct but meaningless.
   Observation: AI focuses on structure, not meaning.

---

4. Challenges Observed

* AI may give logically incorrect answers in complex scenarios
* It may misunderstand ambiguous prompts
* It has limits on response length
* It may generate confident but wrong answers

---

5. Conclusion

This experiment shows that while AI is powerful, it still has limitations in reasoning, ambiguity handling, and edge cases. Careful prompt design is important when working with AI systems.
